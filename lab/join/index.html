How to join the lab?

Members Alumni Interested in joining?

Undergraduates

New students are expected to apply for UROP funding or volunteer for credit. Only current students will be considered for funding directly from Lab. For non-MIT students, consider apply to: Rosetta Commons Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU)

Graduates

The lab cannot take students directly, instead they must apply to one of the MIT PhD programs. Though students can join from department at MIT, the recommended programs (where Sergey has formal affiliation) include: Biology, CSB, Microbiology

Visiting Students

Every year we welcome undergrad and graduate students from around the world to join for the summer or longer. Reach out via contact form to discuss.

Postdocs

Due to limited funding, at this time we expect all new PostDocs to apply for their own funding.

MIT-specific fellowships:

  • Eric and Wendy Schmidt Center (Dec. 22)
  • Tayebati Postdoctoral Fellowship Program
  • MIT-Novo Nordisk Artificial Intelligence Postdoctoral Fellows Program (rolling basis)
  • Biswas Postdoctoral Fellowship Program (Feb. 01)

Non-MIT fellowships:

  • FutureHouse AI-for-Science Independent Postdoctoral Fellowship Program
